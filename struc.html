UPSC Examination Scheme & Subjects

A. Preliminary Examination

Preliminary Examination Overview
Examination Structure The Examination consists of two compulsory papers of 200 marks each.
Paper Type Both question papers are objective type (MCQs), each of two hours duration.
Qualifying Criteria General Studies Paper-II (CSAT) is a qualifying paper with a minimum of 33% marks.
Language[GS 1] Question papers will be set in Hindi and English.
Negative Marking Negative marking is applicable for both GS Paper 1 and GS Paper-II (CSAT). The penalty for an incorrect answer is 1/3rd of the marks assigned to the question.

UPSC Prelims Exam Pattern

Paper Time Duration Total Marks Nature of Paper
General Studies Paper 1 2 hours 200 Objective Type
CSAT (General Studies Paper 2) 2 hours 200 Objective Type

Note: There is negative marking in the Prelims for incorrect answers.
