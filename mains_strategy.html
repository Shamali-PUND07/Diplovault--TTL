UPSC Mains Strategy

üìå Introduction

The UPSC Mains Exam is the second stage of the Civil Services Examination. It consists of nine descriptive papers, including an Essay paper, General Studies papers, and Optional subjects. A structured approach is crucial for success.

üìÖ Study Plan

  • Understand the **UPSC Mains Syllabus** completely.
  • Build conceptual clarity through **NCERTs & standard books**.
  • Focus on **answer writing practice** daily.
  • Make **short notes** for quick revision.
  • Analyze **previous year questions** for pattern recognition.

üìñ Paper-wise Strategy

  • Essay: Practice writing essays on diverse topics.
  • GS 1: History, Geography, Society - use NCERTs & reference books.
  • GS 2: Polity, Governance, International Relations - M. Laxmikanth & newspapers.
  • GS 3: Economy, Science & Tech, Environment - Ramesh Singh, Current Affairs.
  • GS 4: Ethics, Integrity, Aptitude - Solve case studies.
  • Optional Subject: Choose wisely and practice answer writing.

‚è≥ Time Management

Follow a structured timetable:

  • 3-4 months for **GS Papers & Essay**.
  • 2 months for **Optional Subject**.
  • Last 2 months for **Answer Writing & Full-Length Tests**.

üìù Answer Writing & Mock Tests

  • Practice **daily answer writing** for GS & Optional.
  • Follow **TLP (Think-Learn-Practice) approach**.
  • Take **full-length tests** in exam conditions.
  • Review & improve based on **feedback from mentors**.
